Boulan Park Middle School

Michigan

Boulan Park Middle School will be an exemplary learning community supporting creativity, personal responsibility, and innovation. Boulan Park will be a place where a caring and collaborative community, committed to continuous improvement, shares and develops a competitive curriculum and uses cutting-edge instructional strategies and meaningful assessment to ensure accountability and learning for all students.

Life at school

Campuslife
The the life is great

Literacy: 70% of all students will demonstrate a proficiency of 5% (or higher) growth rate in ELA by 6/30/15 as measured on the Informational Text Unit common assessment. Math: All students will show growth in the area of math proficiency. By June 30, 2015, 60-70% of Boulan Park students will achieve 73% or higher on a Troy School District Common Assessment. Science: 80% of All Students will demonstrate a proficiency and achieve a 70% score or higher in Science by 06/30/2015 as measured by the 6th, 7th, and 8th Grade Troy School District Science Common Assessment. Social Studies: 75% of 8th Grade students will demonstrate a proficiency score at 75% or above in persuasive writing in Social Studies by 06/30/2015 as measured by the persuasive writing rubric developed by the Department.
